FINANCIAL BETS

Crown Financial Bets basically offer wagers to predict the exact last digit of the closing indexes around the Asia markets. This would include Odd/Even/Higher or Lower wagers, without the decimal places. The final results and settlement will be governed by the respective stock markets’ official websites. The final result will be declared for the last digit of the closing index in whole number, decimals will not count in any way.

  • The Event shall close earlier or at least one hour before the official market closing time.
  • In the event of a market closing early for that day, irrespective of the circumstances, all bets placed 1 hour before an unscheduled market closing time will be deemed void.
  • In the event when the market is not open, all bets placed on that market for the day will be considered void.
  • In any event which disrupts the market, including the suspension of or limitation of trading by reason of movements in price exceeding limits permitted by the relevant exchange or any other event causing market disruption, Crown may in its absolute discretion with or without notice to you close any or all open bets, refuse any bets, cancel any orders and fill any orders in each case at such level as it may consider in good faith to be appropriate in all the circumstances.
  • The definition of Odd and Even is as follow:
  • Any number ending with 1,3,5,7,9 is ODD.
  • Any number ending with 0,2,4,6,8 is EVEN.
