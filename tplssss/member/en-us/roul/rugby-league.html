RUGBY UNION / RUGBY LEAGUE

Last Update: 25/9/2012

1. GENERAL RULES

  • If the scheduled venue is changed, all bets will be considered void.
  • Unless otherwise stated, all Rugby Union and Rugby League bets are settled based on the scheduled "80 minutes" play.
  • The term "80 minutes" play includes any stoppage time.
  • All In Play bets will be settled based on the outcome at the end of the scheduled "80 minutes" play.
  • Should a game / event be abandoned, suspended or postponed and fail to resume after 12 hours from the official kick off / start time, the result as it stands will be deemed void and all bets will be cancelled, except for those on Markets that have been unconditionally determined. The company's decision to cancel all bets in such an event is final and is regardless of any official decision by the event referee or relevant governing authority.
  • For International matches, as long as the change in venue is within same country, all bets are considered valid.
  • For 1st half betting, bets are settled based on the result derived from the 1st half of the match.
  • For 2nd half betting, bets are settled based on the result derived from the 2nd half of the match, including any extra time.
  • If a match is abandoned during the 1st half, all 1st half bets are considered void. If a match is suspended abandoned during the 2nd half or during extra time, all 2nd half bets are considered void, unless otherwise stated in the individual bet type rules. All 1st half bets will still be valid.

2. BET TYPES

Below is a breakdown of the rules for the individual bet types we offer for Rugby.

2.1. 1 x 2

  • Predict who will win the game. This market will contain the two teams and the draw as betting selections.

2.2. Handicap

  • Predict who will win the game / period with the indicated handicap applied.

2.3. In-Play Handicap

  • Predict who will win the game / period with the indicated handicap applied.
  • Settlement is based on the final score line after the handicap is applied to a 0-0 score line. The current score, at the time of bet placement, is not factored into the bet.

2.4. Over / Under (Points)

  • Predict whether the total number of points scored will be over or under the indicated total line.
  • If a match is abandoned, Over / Under bets will only be settled when the market has been unconditionally determined as any further potential points have no affect on the market result. In all other scenarios, bets will be considered void..

2.5. In-Play Over / Under (Points)

  • Predict whether the total number of points scored will be over or under the indicated total line.
  • Settlement is based on the final score line and the total line is applied to a 0-0 score line. The current score, at the time of bet placement, is not factored into the bet.

2.6. Half Time / Full Time

  • Predict the winning selection at both Half Time and Full Time ("80 Minutes play") in the game.
  • This market is also known as "Double Result"

2.7. Winning Margin

  • This refers to the point difference between the 2 teams at the end of the scheduled "80 minutes" play.
  • If the match is abandoned at any time, all bets are void

2.8. First Try Scorer

  • Predict if the selection will score the first try in the game ("80 Minutes play" only)
  • If the player takes part in the match before the first try has been scored, bets will stand.
  • If the player takes to the field after the first try has been scored, all bets are considered void.
  • If the player does not take part in the match, all bets are considered void.

2.9. Last Try Scorer

  • Predict if the selection will score the first try in the game ("80 Minutes play" only)
  • If the player takes part in the match at any point during the scheduled "80 minutes" play, bets will stand.
  • If the player does not take part in the match, all bets are considered void.

2.10. Anytime Try Scorer

  • Predict if the selection will score a try at anytime during the game ("80 Minutes play" only).
  • If the player takes part in the match at any point during the scheduled "80 minutes" play, bets will stand.
  • If the player does not take part in the match, all bets are considered void.
  • If the match is abandoned after a player has scored, then all bets for that player within the "Anytime Try Scorer" market will be considered valid.

2.11. Team to Score First Try

  • Predict which team will score the first try in the game.
  • Penalty tries do not count and bets apply to the next try.
  • If the game is abandoned at any time, and the first try has already been scored prior to the abandonment, all bets will stand.
  • If no try has been scored at the time of abandonment, all bets are considered void.
  • If no try has been scored after the scheduled "80 minutes" of normal play and stoppage time, then bets are considered void.

2.12. Team to Score Last Try

  • Predict which team will score the last try in the game.
  • Penalty tries do not count and bets apply to the next try.
  • If the game is abandoned at any time, then all bets will be considered void.
  • If no try has been scored after the scheduled "80 minutes" of normal play and stoppage time, then bets are considered void.

2.13. Team to Score First

  • Predict which team will score first in the game. This includes penalty tries.
  • If the game is abandoned at any time, and a team has already been scored prior to the abandonment, all bets will stand.
  • If neither team has scored at the time of abandonment, all bets are considered void.
  • If neither team has scored after the scheduled "80 minutes" of normal play and stoppage time, then bets are considered void.

2.14. Team to Score Last

  • Predict which team will score last in the game. This includes penalty tries.
  • If the game is abandoned at any time, then all bets will be considered void.
  • If neither team has scored after the scheduled "80 minutes" of normal play and stoppage time, then bets are considered void.
